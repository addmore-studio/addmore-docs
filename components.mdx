## Callouts

Five callout types, each for a different severity.

<Note>A note — supplementary information that's safe to skip.</Note>
<Info>An info callout — helpful context, like permissions or prerequisites.</Info>
<Tip>A tip — a recommendation or best practice.</Tip>
<Warning>A warning — something potentially destructive or irreversible.</Warning>
<Check>A check — confirmation that something succeeded.</Check>
