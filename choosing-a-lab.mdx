The right Lab depends on what you're trying to move and what foundation you
already have. This page helps you find the starting point.

## At a glance

| Lab | Best for | Duration | Starts from | Ends with |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Narrative Lab** | Getting your positioning and story clear | 2–4 weeks | €8,500 | A positioning playbook |
| **Launch Lab** | Launching with a full commercial system | 4–8 weeks | On request | A live website + launch assets |
| **Growth Lab** | Improving results after launch | 4–8 weeks / wave | €10–15k / wave | KPI movement, wave by wave |

## Start with Narrative Lab if…

You know **what** you're building but struggle to explain it consistently.

- Preparing for fundraising
- Product-market fit is starting to emerge
- Founder messaging no longer scales
- Sales conversations feel inconsistent
- Your team is growing beyond the founders
- You're preparing for a Launch Lab

## Start with Launch Lab if…

You're approaching a commercial milestone and need to launch with confidence.

- Product launch or fundraise
- New market expansion or repositioning
- A new website is overdue
- Moving into enterprise sales or category creation

<Note>
  Launch Lab already includes the positioning work needed to execute the launch,
  so you don't always need a Narrative Lab first. If the strategic foundation
  still needs real work, start with Narrative Lab.
</Note>

## Start with Growth Lab if…

You already have a commercial foundation and want to keep improving it.

- Launch is complete and you want momentum
- New funding round or market expansion
- A new ICP, product, or enterprise motion to support

<Warning>
  Growth Lab assumes positioning, messaging, and branding already exist. If they
  don't, we'll usually recommend starting with Narrative or Launch Lab.
</Warning>
