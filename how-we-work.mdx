We keep the way we work simple and visible. You should never wonder what's
happening or what comes next. This page covers the principles we hold to and the
weekly rhythm you can expect.

## Principles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ship in small, reviewable steps" icon="git-commit">
    We move in short cycles and share progress often. You see work while it's
    still easy to change, not at the end when it isn't.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Strategy before pixels" icon="target">
    Positioning and messaging come before visual design. A beautiful page for
    the wrong story is wasted work.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="One owner per decision" icon="user-check">
    Every decision has a clear owner on both sides. Shared ownership tends to
    mean no ownership.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Default to writing" icon="pen-line">
    Decisions live in writing so they're searchable, reviewable, and clear long
    after the call ends.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## The weekly rhythm

<Steps>
  <Step title="Monday — plan">
    We agree on the week's focus and the reviewable outcome it should produce.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Midweek — build">
    Heads-down work. We flag blockers early rather than saving them up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Friday — review">
    We walk through the week's progress together and decide what's next.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Async by default, live when it helps. Most updates happen in writing; we
  reserve calls for decisions and reviews.
</Info>

## What we need from you

<Warning>
  Projects slow down most often for one reason: feedback. We need decisions
  within **two business days** to hold the timeline.
</Warning>

- A single point of contact who can make or route decisions
- Access to analytics, brand assets, and any existing research
- Availability for one 30-minute review each week
