We select technology to match the product — not the other way around. The exact
stack depends on the project, but a typical Launch Lab build uses the tools below.

## Typical stack

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Framework" icon="code">
    Next.js for custom builds, Framer for faster marketing sites.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content" icon="database">
    Sanity — a headless CMS your team can manage without us.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hosting & version control" icon="cloud">
    Vercel for hosting and previews, GitHub for source control.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytics" icon="trending-up">
    Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager for measurement and tracking.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## What every build includes

- Responsive implementation
- CMS configuration
- Technical SEO
- Performance optimisation
- Forms
- Analytics and conversion tracking

The objective isn't simply delivering a website — it's delivering a platform your
team can keep building on. Ownership and access are transferred at
[handover](/how-we-work/handover).
