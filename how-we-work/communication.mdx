The way we communicate is the same across every Lab. It's built to keep decisions
moving without filling calendars with unnecessary meetings.

## What every Lab includes

- A dedicated project lead
- Regular progress updates (weekly)
- A shared roadmap
- Slack communication
- Structured review moments
- A final handover

## Async by default

We intentionally work asynchronously wherever possible. Most updates and questions
happen in writing, so decisions can be made quickly and on your own schedule. We
reserve live time for the moments that genuinely benefit from it — kickoffs,
reviews, and key decisions.

<Note>
  Cadence differs slightly by Lab: Launch and Narrative Labs run on weekly
  progress updates and structured review windows; Growth Lab runs a weekly
  check-in with an end-of-wave review.
</Note>
