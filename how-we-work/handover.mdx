A Lab doesn't end when the work goes live. Every engagement concludes with a
structured handover. The objective is full autonomy — your team should understand
how everything works without depending on us for every future update.

## What handover includes

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Training" icon="graduation-cap">
    CMS training and a full walkthrough of the website or deliverables.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Documentation" icon="book-open">
    How everything is built and how to maintain it after we step back.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Access & ownership" icon="key">
    Transfer of accounts, design files, and repository ownership where applicable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Asset delivery" icon="package">
    Final launch assets and source files, organised and handed over.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## You own what we build

Everything we deliver is built for client ownership. There are no licenses to
renew and nothing is locked to us — you can manage and extend it independently.

<Info>
  Prefer to keep us involved? Many clients continue with [Growth Lab](/growth-lab)
  for ongoing work — but it's never required to keep what you have running.
</Info>
