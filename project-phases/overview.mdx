Every engagement moves through the same four phases. The length of each depends
on scope, but the order never changes — each phase produces what the next one
needs.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Discovery" icon="search" href="/project-phases/discovery">
    Learn the product, market, and customers. Define what success looks like.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Design" icon="pen-tool" href="/project-phases/design">
    Turn strategy into messaging, structure, and visual design.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build" icon="code" href="/project-phases/build">
    Engineer the site or product surface, reviewed in short cycles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Launch" icon="rocket" href="/project-phases/launch">
    Ship, measure, and hand off everything you need to keep moving.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## At a glance

| Phase | Typical length | You'll receive |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Discovery | 1–2 weeks | Positioning brief, success metrics |
| Design | 2–4 weeks | Messaging, wireframes, visual design |
| Build | 3–6 weeks | Production site or product surface |
| Launch | 1 week | Live release, handoff docs, measurement plan |

<Note>
  Timelines are indicative. We confirm the actual schedule during kickoff once
  scope is set.
</Note>
