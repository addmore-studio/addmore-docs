Discovery is where we build the shared understanding everything else rests on.
We learn your product, your market, and your customers, then agree on what a
successful outcome actually looks like.

## What happens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Kickoff session">
    A single working session to align on goals, scope, and constraints.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Research">
    We review your analytics, talk to your team, and study the competitive set.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Positioning">
    We draft a positioning brief — who you're for, and why you win.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Success metrics">
    We agree on the numbers this project should move.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What we'll ask you for

<Info>
  The more context you share early, the sharper the strategy. Nothing here needs
  to be polished — raw notes are fine.
</Info>

- Access to analytics and any past research
- Introductions to two or three customers, if possible
- Current messaging, brand assets, and sales decks

<Check>
  Discovery ends when we've signed off on the positioning brief together.
</Check>
