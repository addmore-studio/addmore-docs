Design turns the positioning brief into something you can see. We work from
words to structure to visuals, in that order, so the design carries the story
rather than decorating it.

## What happens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Messaging">
    We write the core narrative — headline, value props, and page-level copy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wireframes">
    We map structure and flow before any visual design, so we're arguing about
    the right things early.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visual design">
    We apply your brand and design the pages to production fidelity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reviews

<Tip>
  Bring reactions, not rewrites. Tell us what isn't landing and why — we'll take
  it from there. That tends to produce better work than line edits.
</Tip>

Each deliverable gets one structured review round. We consolidate feedback into a
single pass to avoid the churn of endless small edits.

<Warning>
  Visual design starts only after messaging and wireframes are signed off.
  Changing the story late is the most expensive thing we can do.
</Warning>
