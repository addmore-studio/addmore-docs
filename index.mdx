addmore is a product marketing and design/dev studio. We work mostly with B2B
SaaS teams who need sharper positioning, a site that converts, and the design and
engineering to ship it.

We deliver that work through three engagements — our **Labs**. This is the
working manual for each one: what it is, when to use it, what you receive, and how
we run it together.

## The three Labs

## How the Labs fit together

The Labs are designed as a progression. Most companies move through them in order,
but each one stands on its own.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Narrative Lab — get clear">
    Define what you do, why it matters, and how to say it. The positioning system
    everything else builds on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Lab — go to market">
    Turn that positioning into brand, website, and launch assets — the commercial
    system you launch with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Growth Lab — keep moving">
    After launch, run consecutive waves to improve conversion, expand markets, and
    ship new initiatives.
  </Step>
</Steps>
