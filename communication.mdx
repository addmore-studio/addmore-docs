Good communication is mostly about being predictable. This page sets out where
conversations happen, how quickly to expect replies, and who to go to for what.

## Channels

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Slack">
    Day-to-day questions, quick updates, and anything async. This is the default
    for most conversations. Expect a reply within a few working hours.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email">
    Anything that needs a paper trail — approvals, scope changes, invoices.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Calls">
    Reserved for decisions and reviews. Always scheduled, always with an agenda,
    always followed by written notes.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Response times

| Channel | Expect a reply within |
| --- | --- |
| Slack | A few working hours |
| Email | One business day |
| Urgent (flagged) | Same day |

<Warning>
  Please keep feedback and approvals in one place per deliverable. Scattered
  comments across email, Slack, and docs are the easiest way to lose a decision.
</Warning>
