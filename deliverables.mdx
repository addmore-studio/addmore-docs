Every phase produces concrete, reviewable deliverables. Nothing is theoretical —
you can always point to what exists and what's next.

## By phase

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Discovery">
    - Positioning brief
    - Competitive landscape summary
    - Agreed success metrics
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Design">
    - Core messaging and page copy
    - Wireframes for every page
    - Production-fidelity visual design
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Build">
    - Production site or product surface
    - Live preview environment
    - Quality report (performance, accessibility)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Launch">
    - Live production release
    - Handoff documentation
    - Measurement plan
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Ownership

<Info>
  You own every deliverable outright. There are no licenses to renew and nothing
  is locked to us.
</Info>

<Accordion title="What about ongoing changes after handoff?" icon="wrench">
  Your team can maintain everything independently. If you'd rather we stay on for
  ongoing work, we can set up a separate retainer — but it's never required to
  keep what you have running.
</Accordion>
